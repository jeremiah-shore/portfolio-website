About Me

Hello! My name is Jeremiah Shore. I've been working professionally on websites, apps, and games for 6+ years.

I was born in central PA, raised in WV, and I currently live in Denver, CO.

I went to school for computer networking and information technology; I have associate's degrees in both. I worked in IT for 8 years before changing to software development full-time.

I currently work as a software engineer at Zicasso.

When I'm not coding or designing, I'm usually working on my music, performing at open mics, or spending time with friends. I also enjoy playing and making video games.

Due to COVID-19 and its effects on the travel industry, I'm currently looking for work as a full-stack Java developer in the greater Denver, CO area. I'm also seeking freelance projects to apply and expand my skills as a designer and developer.
